How to Deposit & Withdraw

Deposit Process

To deposit money into your account, follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the "Deposit" page.
  • Choose your preferred payment method and enter the amount you wish to deposit.
  • Make the payment to the provided account and note the transaction ID (TrxID).
  • Upload a screenshot of the payment and submit your request.
  • The admin will verify your payment and add the amount to your balance.

Withdraw Process

To withdraw money from your account, follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the "Withdraw" page.
  • Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and select your preferred withdrawal method.
  • Submit your withdrawal request.
  • The admin will approve your request and process your payment to the provided account.
